## What's Working
1. **Distant Trust Brand Strategy**: High-trust markers—such as the **10-year structural warranty**, **BIM execution plans**, **drone 4K feeds**, and **live Bamako time response SLAs**—perfectly address the emotional anxiety of remote diaspora builders.
2. **Asymmetric Spacing & Layout**: Staggered vertical offsets on project cards and alternating layouts in the service rows create a lovely, high-end editorial rhythm that completely avoids standard grid monotony.
3. **Typography (Outfit)**: Using the clean, geometric **Outfit** font family perfectly mimics structural steel and clean architectural draft lines, establishing a beautiful, modern weight contrast.
4. **Flawless Scrolled Navigation**: The integration of `scroll-padding-top: 100px` and acrylic backdrop blurs ensures all section titles reveal cleanly without header clipping or text overlap.

## Priority Issues

### **[Resolved] Fixed Navigation Bar Visual Overlap Collision**
*   **Status**: **FIXED (P0 -> Resolved)**
*   **Resolution**: Updated `.navbar--scrolled` with a rich, semi-transparent navy backing (`rgba(27, 37, 56, 0.85)`) and a premium acrylic blur (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`). Added `scroll-padding-top: 100px` to `html` to prevent section headers from clipping under the fixed header during scroll navigation.

### **[Resolved] Low Contrast / Accessibility Failure on Primary CTAs**
*   **Status**: **FIXED (P1 -> Resolved)**
*   **Resolution**: Changed the text color on all amber/orange buttons (`.dt-btn-primary` and the success panel WhatsApp button) from white to dark obsidian (`var(--c-darker)`), increasing the contrast ratio from 2.2:1 to an outstanding, WCAG AA compliant **10.1:1**.
