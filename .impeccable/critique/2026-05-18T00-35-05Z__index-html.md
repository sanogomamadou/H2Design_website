## Overall Impression
The website represents a highly refined, premium monograph for a high-end Malian construction agency. The use of asymmetric vertical rhythm, generous white space, and structural solidity is exceptionally well-suited for H2 Design's target market (diaspora property owners). 

However, **two critical implementation flaws completely break this premium experience**:
1. Severe visual collisions caused by a lack of solid backing/backdrop-blur on the fixed navigation bar during scrolling.
2. An extreme accessibility failure on the primary CTA buttons, where white text sits on a medium-light amber/orange background.

Fixing these two bugs will elevate this site from a flawed prototype to an editorial-grade, accessible masterpiece.

## Priority Issues

### **[P0] Fixed Navigation Bar Visual Overlap Collision**
*   **Why it matters**: As the user scrolls, underlying headings (e.g., "SUIVI À DISTANCE", "Rénovation") slide directly under the fixed header. Because the navbar background lacks proper solid color or backdrop-blur styling, the underlying texts and header elements collide, rendering the navigation bar links and scrolled section titles completely illegible and visually chaotic.
*   **Fix**: Update `.navbar--scrolled` and `.navbar` in [style.css](file:///c:/Users/info/Desktop/DIGICRAFT/SiteWeb/H2_Design_Website/src/style.css) to have a solid background color (`var(--c-darker)` or deep navy `var(--c-navy)`) or apply a highly premium acrylic blur using `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` and `-webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`.
*   **Suggested command**: `layout`

### **[P1] Low Contrast / Accessibility Failure on Primary CTAs**
*   **Why it matters**: The primary CTA buttons (e.g., `"Accès Espace Client"` / `.dt-btn-primary`) feature **White text on an Amber/Orange background (`#E8A33D`)**. This combination yields an extremely low contrast ratio of **2.2:1**, failing the WCAG AA requirement of **4.5:1**. This makes the primary action invisible or extremely difficult to read for visually impaired users.
*   **Fix**: Change the text color of all elements sitting on the amber/orange background to deep brand navy (`var(--c-navy)`) or dark obsidian (`var(--c-darker)`), achieving a robust contrast ratio of over **5.5:1**.
*   **Suggested command**: `colorize`

### **[P1] Viewport Section Entrance Clipping**
*   **Why it matters**: Active content sections (e.g., About, Services, Projects) scroll directly underneath the fixed/colliding header, causing the top ~80px of section headers to be obscured or cropped during transition reveals.
*   **Fix**: Adjust scroll-margin/scroll-padding behaviors or increase vertical scroll paddings in [style.css](file:///c:/Users/info/Desktop/DIGICRAFT/SiteWeb/H2_Design_Website/src/style.css) to ensure proper vertical breathing room.
*   **Suggested command**: `layout`
