# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Individual homeowners and property owners, with a significant segment (30%) being Malians living abroad (diaspora). These users are looking for trustworthy partners to renovate or build new homes in Mali remotely.

## Product Purpose
To serve as the primary digital touchpoint for H2 Design, a construction agency. The goal is to generate leads and build absolute trust with potential clients, particularly those managing projects from afar, by showcasing past realizations, transparent processes, and architectural excellence.

## Brand Personality
The Reliable Master Builder. The tone is authoritative, sturdy, transparent, and trustworthy. It should evoke confidence, security, and a sense of "Distant Trust" for remote clients.

## Anti-references
Generic, template-driven construction agency websites. We are avoiding standard SaaS looks or cheap, cluttered layouts.

## Design Principles
1. **Radical Transparency**: Show, don't just tell. Use high-quality imagery of actual projects and clear explanations of the process to prove reliability.
2. **Distant Trust**: Every element must reinforce security and professionalism to reassure diaspora clients that their investment is safe.
3. **Modern Solidity**: The interface should feel as structurally sound and thoughtfully planned as the buildings H2 Design creates.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
- Optimized for varying bandwidths (crucial for users in Mali or those with unstable connections).
- High contrast and highly legible typography.
